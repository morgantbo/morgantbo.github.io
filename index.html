About Me

Hello! My name is Morgan! I am a twenty years old from Long Island, New York and I'm a Junior of The University of Michigan's BFA Acting Program with an intended Computer Science minor through the College of Literature, Science, and the Arts.

At U-M, I’ve been fortunate to perform in plays such as Love and Information, Grapes of Wrath, and A Beautiful Country. In addition to theatre performance, I have a love for Hip-Hop that led me to be part of my school’s dance team (@michigandance2XS), a UMICH chapter of the international hip hop dance company, Dance2XS, and a few Graduate student dance performances.

In addition, I have a knack for Computer Science, including but not limited to a robust knowledge in Python and C++. After completion of my very first introductory computer science course offered at U-M, I was able to not only learn how to build several projects including a simulated elevator game, but I was also given strong foundational skills on how to approach programming problems. Never in a million years would I find myself learning how to code in a virtual/remote classroom setting!

Outside of performance, I love to read, work out, listen to or play music, and cook! You can either find me on a) a nice patch of grass near some tall trees b) on my skateboard to grab my second cup of coffee for the day c) at the gym!
